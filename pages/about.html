About Us

Welcome to Home recommendation webpage, a smart house recommendation platform designed to simplify your search for the perfect home. Our project is developed as part of our Design Engineering coursework, reflecting our commitment to innovation and user-centric design in real estate technology.

Our platform provides personalized house and flat recommendations by analyzing location preferences, budget constraints, and other key factors. Whether you're a first-time buyer, a renter, or looking to relocate, we aim to make your property search more efficient and tailored to your needs.

We are a team of enthusiastic students with a passion for solving real-world problems through creative technology solutions. This project embodies our skills in web development, data analysis, and user interface design to deliver a seamless user experience. Thank you for visiting our Webpage.
